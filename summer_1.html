      Concordia Class Schedule Builder
      Hi, Tymofiy Kovalenko. The Concordia Class Schedule Builder is a tool to assist you to generate a workable personalized class schedule with ease based on the published class schedule. Note that the tool does not register you for any courses, as further outlined below.


      Please read the following points carefully before proceeding to Class Schedule Builder:
      • Before you begin to build your schedule please ensure that you have consulted the Academic Calendar and understand the requirements for your program of study. The Class Schedule Builder does not validate whether you are eligible to take the classes that you select. It is your responsibility to ensure that you meet any requisites or other enrollment restrictions for the classes.
      • Successfully generating a schedule via Class Schedule Builder does not mean you are actually registered in the classes that you have selected. You MUST complete your registration through your Student Centre.
      • The Class Schedule Builder is NOT meant to replace academic advising or your advisement report. If you still have questions about which classes to take, you should speak to the appropriate advisor. You can find the names and contact information of your advisors on the right side of your Student Centre homepage.
